The Story Behind Revamped

Revamped Agencies was built out of a simple belief: that the life insurance industry was overdue for a serious upgrade. Too many agents were being handed a contract, thrown into the deep end, and left to figure it out alone. We decided to change that.

We've built an agency from the ground up that actually invests in its people — with real training, real leads, and a real community behind every agent. Our focus is equal parts client protection and agent development, because when our agents thrive, families get better coverage and better service.

This is what a modern agency looks like. We built it. Now we're growing it.

Rooted in Integrity

We do right by clients and agents alike. Every recommendation we make, every agent we bring on — it starts with honesty and ends with results.

Obsessed with Performance

We measure what matters. Our systems, our culture, and our coaching are all built around one goal: helping our agents produce at the highest level possible.

Built for the Digital Age

Our agents work from anywhere. Our tech, our leads, and our training infrastructure are all designed for a world where geography isn't a limitation.

🎯

Client-First Coverage

Every client we work with gets straightforward guidance, honest recommendations, and a policy built around their actual needs — not a sales quota.

💻

Work From Anywhere

Our entire operation is built to run virtually. Agents across the country close deals, attend training, and grow their business without ever being tied to a desk or office.

🚀

No-Fluff Execution

We skip the corporate nonsense and focus on what actually produces results — sharp systems, direct coaching, and a team that holds a high standard across the board.

🤝

Mutual Growth

When our agents win, clients get better service and our agency grows stronger. We've structured everything so those outcomes are inseparable.

Our Mission

Protect Families.
Develop Agents.

Revamped Agencies exists to close two gaps at once: the gap between families and the financial protection they need, and the gap between agents and the income they're capable of earning.

We've built a fully remote operation that puts the right tools, the right leads, and the right coaching in front of every agent from day one. No hunting for prospects, no winging your pitch — just a clear path and a team behind you every step of the way.

Our standard is simple: do right by every client, develop every agent to their full potential, and never stop raising the bar.
